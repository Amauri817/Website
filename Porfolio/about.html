About

My name is Amauri Brown and I am currently a senior at Michigan State University, studying Games and Interactive Media. I am from Detroit, Michigan and have a passion for designing the technical functions within video games and strive to create innovative game mechanics. I work to add something new and unique in every project I create to help build my experience, and my understanding of games.
